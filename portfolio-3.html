Care Connect

Care Connect is a comprehensive web application designed to facilitate seamless communication and treatment management between doctors and patients. Developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, PHP, and MySQL, Care Connect offers a user-friendly interface for managing medical appointments, prescriptions, and patient records.

With Care Connect, doctors can efficiently schedule appointments, view patient medical histories, prescribe medications, and provide remote consultations. Patients can easily book appointments, access their treatment plans, communicate with their doctors, and receive reminders for medication schedules.

Care Connect ensures a secure and privacy-focused experience for both doctors and patients. It includes features such as secure login/authentication, encrypted data transmission, and role-based access control to safeguard sensitive medical information. Additionally, Care Connect offers a responsive design, allowing users to access the application from any device, whether desktop or mobile.
